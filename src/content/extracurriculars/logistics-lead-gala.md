## Overview
As the Logistics Lead for the Gala, I was responsible for the operational backbone of the school's most prestigious event, managing a venue for over 200 guests.

## Key Responsibilities
* **Venue & Vendor Management:** Sourced reception halls and negotiated contracts with caterers and security.
* **Transport Strategy:** Orchestrated the shuttle logistics for 100+ attendees to ensure safe and efficient movement.
* **Pricing Strategy:** Established the ticket pricing tiers and managed beverage stocks to optimize the event budget.



> "This role sharpened my ability to make autonomous decisions under high pressure during the live event execution."
